The Gameboy Zero is the attempt to put inside a classic Gameboy case a Raspberry Pi Zero with all required extras to run a Retropie OS and made it possible to play virtually any game from Atari 2600 to PS1 at any place.

Gameboy case with some of the parts that hopefully someday will be together and working.

This is not an original idea at all, in fact there are dozens of Gameboys Zero around the web.

Despite the fact that it looks relativelly simple and straightforward to do I'm facing a lot o problems to make it work. For example, I have 3 LCD screens and each one imposes a difficult and I'm yet to choose between them.

One invaluable source of information on this matter is the Sudomod community.
